OpenClub Privacy Policy


  1. Background
    1. OpenClub Pty Ltd (ACN 615 034 752) and OpenClub Holdings Pty Ltd (ACN 614 978 639) (‘OpenClub’) is committed to ensuring your privacy through compliance with thePrivacy Act 1988 (Cth) (‘ Privacy Act’) and the Australian Privacy Principles (‘APPs’).
    2. This Privacy Policy (the ‘Policy’) applies to all personal information collected by OpenClub via the website located at www.OpenClub.co and all its applicable sub-domains, domains and its digital applications (the ‘Services’). This Policy should be read in conjunction with either the Club Engagement Agreement or the Member Agreement where applicable.
    3. Your privacy is very important to us. For that reason, please read the following details carefully and contact OpenClub if you have any questions.
  2. What personal information is collected?
    1. OpenClub, in the course of conducting its business, collects a range of personal information including, amongst other things, names, email addresses, mailing and residential addresses, financial and credit card information, business and home phone numbers.
    2. OpenClub may also collect your device ID, device type, geo-location information, computer and connection information, statistics on page views, traffic to and from the sites, ad data, IP address and standard web log information;
    3. OpenClub may also collect cookies from your computer, which tells OpenClub when you visit and use the Services as well as remembering your preferences.
    4. OpenClub only collects personal information that is reasonably necessary for one or more of OpenClub’s functions or activities.
    5. OpenClub only collects sensitive information if it is reasonably necessary for OpenClub functions or activities, or an exception applies under the Privacy Act and the individual to whom the information relates has given their express consent to the collection.
    6. You can choose not to provide certain information, however, if you do not provide OpenClub with certain types of personal information, you may not be able to have full enjoyment of the Services.
  3. How is personal information collected?
    1. Collection of personal information by OpenClub is only done by lawful and fair means.
    2. OpenClub collects personal information about an individual directly from that individual wherever it is reasonable and practicable to do so.
    3. Collection of personal information may be through online competitions, emails, the OpenClub Services, receipts, telephone conversations, consultations and attendance at events.
    4. OpenClub will always take reasonable steps to ensure individuals are aware of (amongst other things) the facts and circumstances of collection, the purpose of collection and information about this Policy.
    5. OpenClub may also use cookies to collect data that may include personal information. For example, where a cookie is linked to your account, it will be considered personal information under the Privacy Act. OpenClub will handle any personal information collected by cookies in the same manner as all other personal information as described in this Policy.
  4. How is personal information used by OpenClub and under what circumstances will it be disclosed?
    1. OpenClub will only use or disclose personal information for a purpose for which it was collected (or for a secondary purpose if an exception applies), including things such as managing its relationships, performing administrative functions, informing customers and others about content and services (whether by direct marketing methods or otherwise), market research, building and maintaining business relationships, implementing its communication model and complying with legal requirements.
    2. Where OpenClub uses personal information other than for a purpose for which it was collected, OpenClub does so in accordance with the Privacy Act and APP 6.
    3. OpenClub may disclose personal information to its professional advisors (including lawyers) and contracted third party service providers and partners, including marketing and advertising agencies and information technology service providers. However, OpenClub will never disclose your personal information to an unknown third party, unless you have given your consent to such a disclosure.
    4. OpenClub may disclose your personal information to a trusted third party who also holds other information about you. This third party may combine that information in order to enable it and us to develop anonymised consumer insights so that OpenClub can better understand your preferences and interests, personalise your experience and enhance the products and services that you receive.
  5. Who does OpenClub Disclose your information to?
    1. OpenClub may disclose your personal information for the purposes described in this policy to:
      1. related bodies corporate and employees of OpenClub;
      2. OpenClub’s existing or potential agents, business partners or partners;
      3. professional advisers, dealers and agents;
      4. payment systems operators;
      5. third party suppliers and service providers (including providers for the operation of OpenClub’s Services and/or business or in connection with providing OpenClub products and Services to you);
      6. anyone to whom OpenClub’s assets or businesses (or any part of them) are transferred;
      7. our sponsors or promoters of any competition that we conduct via the Services;
      8. specific third parties authorised by you to receive information held by OpenClub; and/or
      9. other persons, including government agencies, regulatory bodies and law enforcement agencies, or as required, authorised or permitted by law.
  6. Direct Marketing
    1. OpenClub may engage in direct marketing of its services from time to time.
    2. OpenClub only uses personal information for direct marketing in circumstances where OpenClub has collected the information directly from the individual and that individual reasonably expects to receive direct marketing material from OpenClub (for example, where an individual has signed up to receive direct marketing material from OpenClub).
    3. OpenClub offers all individuals the opportunity to unsubscribe from receiving direct marketing communications.
  7. Access to and accuracy of personal information
    1. OpenClub takes all reasonable steps to ensure that the personal information it collects, uses and/or discloses is accurate, complete, up to date and relevant.
    2. You have the right to ensure that your personal information which is held by OpenClub is accurate. To make a request to access and/or correct your personal information, please contact OpenClub’s Privacy Officer using the contact details provided below.
    3. While OpenClub will always endeavour to give access to personal information free of charge, OpenClub reserves the right to charge individuals for access to personal information to cover costs associated with:
      1. searching for, locating and retrieving personal information and deciding which information to provide to the individual;
      2. using an intermediary; and
      3. reproducing and sending the information.
    4. OpenClub is entitled to refuse to give access to personal information in certain circumstances. For further information, please contact OpenClub’s Privacy Officer using the contact details provided below.
  8. How secure is your personal information?
    1. OpenClub takes all reasonable steps to ensure that information held by OpenClub is safe and secure, and that it is protected from misuse, loss, unauthorised access, modification and disclosure.
    2. While OpenClub takes all reasonable steps to ensure the security of your personal information, data protection and security measures can never be guaranteed and, accordingly, OpenClub cannot guarantee the security of your personal information.
  9. Destruction and de-identification of personal information
    1. OpenClub will take reasonable steps to de-identify or destroy personal information when it is no longer needed by OpenClub, except where the information forms part of a Commonwealth record or OpenClub is required, by law or a court/tribunal order, to retain the information.
  10. Anonymity and pseudonymity
    1. Individuals have the option of dealing anonymously or by pseudonym with OpenClub unless it is impracticable to do so or OpenClub is required, by law or a court or tribunal order, to deal with identified individuals.
  11. Overseas disclosure of your information
    1. OpenClub may disclose your personal information outside of Australia to entities of the kind listed in paragraph 6 of this Policy.
    2. By providing your personal information to us, you consent to the disclosure of your information outside of Australia and acknowledge Australian Privacy Principle 8.1, as outlined in Schedule 1 to the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), will not apply to the use of the information.
    3. You further acknowledge that OpenClub is not responsible for privacy practices of the overseas entities they may disclose your information to and acknowledge that OpenClub are not required to ensure that overseas recipients handle that personal information in compliance with Australian Privacy Law.
    4. OpenClub will, however, take reasonable steps to ensure that any overseas recipient will deal with such personal information in a way that is consistent with the Australian Privacy Principles.
  12. Third Party Links
    1. The Services may contain links to websites or products operated by third parties.
    2. Those links are provided for convenience and may not remain current or be maintained. Unless expressly stated otherwise, OpenClub are not responsible for the privacy practices of, or any content on, those linked websites or products, and have no control over or rights in those linked websites.
    3. The privacy policies that apply to those other websites may differ substantially from the Policy, so we encourage individuals to read them before using those websites or products.
  13. Changes to the Policy
    1. OpenClub reserves the right to amend this Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on the Services.
    2. You should regularly check the Services to see if there have been any recent changes.
  14. Complaints
    1. If you believe there has been a breach of the APPs or an APP Code, please contact OpenClub’s Privacy Officer using the details provided below.
    2. OpenClub takes privacy complaints very seriously. If you make a complaint, OpenClub will respond within give (5) calendar days to advise you who is responsible for managing your complaint. OpenClub will try to resolve your complaint within ten (10) calendar days. When this is not possible, OpenClub will contact you within that time to let you know how long OpenClub will take to resolve your complaint.
    3. OpenClub will investigate your complaint and, where necessary, consult with third parties about your complaint. OpenClub will make a decision about your complaint and write to you to explain your decision.
    4. Individuals can also complain directly to the Office of the Australian Privacy Commissioner. Details about how to file a complaint can be found at www.oaic.gov.au or by calling 1300 363 992.
