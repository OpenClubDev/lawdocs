1. Definitions

In this Club Engagement Agreement:

ACCC means the Australian Competition and Consumer Commission.

Agent Fee means the fee agreed between OpenClub and the Club for providing the Agent Services, being a 5% of the Member Payment exclusive of GST.

Agent Services means the services provided for in the Club Engagement Agreement.

Australian Consumer Law means Competition and Consumer Act 2010 (Cth) Schedule 2.

Business Day means a day that is not a Saturday, Sunday or public holiday in Brisbane, Queensland.

Club means the club, association, social groups or any other group of individuals with a common purpose, party to this Club Engagement Agreement.

Club Engagement Agreement means this agreement establishing a relationship of principal and agent between the Club and OpenClub.

Club Terms and Conditions means the terms and conditions of the Club, for providing the Membership Services, or a similar agreement.

Force Majeure Event means an act of God, fire, terrorism, riot, strike, lock-out or other industrial action which delays or prevents a party from performing its obligations under this Club Engagement Agreement.

GST means the goods and services tax imposed by:

  1. A New Tax System (Goods and Services Tax) Act 1999 (Cth), the rate of which (currently 10%) is multiplied to any payment (or the relevant part thereof); or
  2. if that Act does not exist for any reason, any Act imposing or relating to the imposition or administration of a goods and services tax in Australia and any regulation made under that Act.

Insolvency Event means a liquidation or winding up, the appointment of a controller, administrator, receiver, manager or similar insolvency administrator to a party or any substantial part of its assets or the occurrence of any event that has a substantially similar effect to any of the above events.

Intellectual Property means all rights conferred under statute, common law or equity, wherever in the world subsisting, in relation to trade marks, copyright, patents, patentable inventions and designs, whether existing now or in the future and whether or not registered or registrable, and includes any rights subsisting in or relating to trade secrets, know how, inventions, discoveries, geographical indications of origin, semiconductor and circuit layouts, programming tools, object code, source code, microcode, mask works, methods, techniques, recipes, formulae, algorithms, modules, libraries and databases and further includes the right to apply for the registration or grant of any such intellectual property.

Liability Cap means an amount equal to the aggregated total of the Agent Fees retained by OpenClub in the six (6) calendar months prior to the event causing the loss suffered by the Club.

Membership means the membership agreement entered into by the Member and the Club.

Member means the party to the Membership receiving the Membership Services.

Member Data means data inputted by the Member in the course of using the Membership Services, which may include their Personal Information.

Member Payment means any payment received by OpenClub, on behalf of the Club, made by a Member for any good or services provided as part of the Membership Services.

Member Refund means any payment agreed or required to be paid by OpenClub to the Member, on behalf of the Club, as a refund or partial refund of a Member Payment.

Membership Services means the membership package:

  1. provided by the Club; and
  2. supplied to the Member,

included in the Membership.

OpenClub means OpenClub Pty Ltd ACN 615 034 752.

Personal Information means information or an opinion about an identified individual, or an individual who is reasonably identifiable:

  1. whether the information or opinion is true or not; and
  2. whether the information or opinion is recorded in a material form or not,

which is provided to or otherwise comes into OpenClub’s possession as a result of this Agreement.

Prescribed Event means any event in which the services provided for in that event:

  1. contravene any law or regulation in Australia;
  2. facilitate the contravention of any law or regulation in Australia;
  3. infringe any Intellectual Property rights of OpenClub; or
  4. take place in any country subject to a goods or services embargo, declared by the Australian Federal Government, from time to time.

Prescribed Organisation means any organisation:

  1. located, domiciled or carrying out activities in any country subject to a goods or services embargo, declared by the Australian Federal Government, from time to time;
  2. subject to the Australian Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade Consolidated List;
  3. subject to sanctions in any other country; or
  4. listed on any terminated merchant databases, including, but not limited to, the relevant databases of MasterCard and Visa.

Service Fee means the amount due and payable to be remitted to the Club, limited to the Member Payment reduced by the Agent Fee.

Website means www.openclub.co and any website, platform, software or downloadable application operated by OpenClub or its Related Bodies Corporate, from time to time.

  1. The Parties agree to enter into a limited authorised Club Engagement Agreement.
  2. Unless otherwise agreed, the authorised activity and agent services of OpenClub, in its role as agent under this Club Engagement Agreement, is limited to:
  1. maintaining a page exclusively dedicated to each Club on the Website;
  2. managing member registrations on the Club’s behalf;
  3. promoting the events, goods or services of the Club;
  4. provide a facility for the execution of agreements, whether electronic or otherwise, between Members and the Club;
  5. facilitating the ability for the Club to maintain the Membership Services on the Website;
  6. receiving payments from the Member on behalf of the Club;
  7. acting as agent of the Club regarding correspondence relating to the Membership;
  8. remitting payments to the Club; and
  9. issuing refunds to the Member, if necessary, at the direction of the Club.
  1. OpenClub Obligations
  1. On receipt of payment of the Member Payment, OpenClub agrees to remit the Service Fee to the Club, pursuant to the terms of the Club Engagement Agreement.
  2. Remittance of the Service Fee, referred to in clause 4(a), will be processed within five (5) Business Days of receiving a Member Payment into OpenClub’s bank account or other account used for receiving Member Payments.
  3. A Member Refund will be paid by OpenClub to the Member within five (5) Business Days of OpenClub receiving:
    1. cleared funds, equal to the amount of the Member Refund, paid by the Club, into OpenClub’s nominated bank; and
    2. written notice from the Club to issue the Member Refund.
  4. A written notice issued pursuant to clause 4(c)(ii) must include adequate information to allow OpenClub to process the refund efficiently and effectively.
  5. For the avoidance of doubt, in the event a refund is to be issued to the Member:
  1. OpenClub will retain the Service Fee; and
  2. It is the responsibility of the Club to pay to the Member the full refund amount:
  1. agreed between the Club and the Member; or
  2. as required by law.
